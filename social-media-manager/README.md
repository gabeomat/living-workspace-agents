# Social Media Manager

**Give it one piece of long-form content. Get a full week of on-brand social posts — written, designed, and scheduled.**

Drop in a transcript, a video, a podcast, or a post, and this agent turns it into a whole week of Instagram + Facebook content: written in your voice, rendered as branded image cards and a Reel, and loaded into your scheduler as drafts. It stops and shows you everything before it builds a single graphic.

## What happens when you run it

1. **First run only — it builds your look.** A quick design onboarding: you show it a few example images of the aesthetic you want, and it creates your **Social Design System** — your colors, fonts, layouts, weekly post mix, and scheduler. After that, every run just reads it.
2. **It mines your content.** Pulls the teachable moments, quotes, and hooks out of your long-form piece.
3. **It writes the week in your voice.** A mix of posts (by default: several static image cards, a couple of carousels, and a Reel script) — captions and on-image copy, all in your voice.
4. **You approve — hard stop.** It shows you every caption and every slide's text *before* building anything. Nothing gets designed until you say go. Edit or approve.
5. **It designs the graphics.** Renders your branded image cards + a Reel as real image/video files, styled to your design system.
6. **It schedules them as drafts.** Loads the week into your scheduler (Metricool by default; adapts to others, or hands you a ready-to-post schedule).

## What you end up with
A full week of on-brand, in-your-voice social posts — written, designed as finished graphics + a Reel, and sitting as drafts in your scheduler, ready for your final look before they go live.

## What you need
- **Claude** with this skill installed, and a few **example images** on first run so it can learn your look.
- **Recommended — a brand-voice skill** so captions sound like you.
- **To render the graphics:** Playwright + ffmpeg. In Claude Code / the desktop app these install automatically the first time (you just approve). Only a locked-down environment can't render — then it hands you the finished HTML to render elsewhere.
- **To auto-schedule:** a connector like Metricool. Without one, it hands you the finished posts + a posting schedule.

## Honest limits
It schedules **drafts**, not live posts — you keep the final publish decision. Auto-rendering and auto-scheduling depend on the tools above; without them you still get all the copy and design, just with a manual finish.
