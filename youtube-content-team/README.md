# YouTube Content Team

**One command takes you from "what should I make a video about?" to a finished script and two branded thumbnails with your face in them.**

This is a six-person YouTube team compressed into one agent: a trend analyst, a strategist, a director, a hook writer, a scriptwriter, and a thumbnail designer. It researches what's working in your niche, picks the best idea for *your* business, writes the script in your voice, and designs the thumbnails.

## What happens when you run it

1. **First run only — it sets up your channel.** A quick onboarding: you drop 2–4 of your own headshots (varied expressions) into a folder and tell it your thumbnail-brand look. It saves this as your profile so every future video is one command.
2. **Scout — it researches trends.** Finds what's trending in your niche (via web search out of the box; or a real YouTube-API feed if you set one up) and delivers a briefing.
3. **Strategist — 5 ideas.** Generates five video ideas tailored to your audience and business.
4. **Director — picks the best.** Scores them against your goals and timing, picks the one to make, and tells you why.
5. **Hook Writer — nails the opening.** Writes three hook options and selects the strongest.
6. **Script Writer — the full script.** A complete, filmable script in your voice — intro, body, CTA, outro — saved to a file.
7. **Thumbnail Designer — two branded thumbnails.** Creates two thumbnail variations using your brand colors and *your real face* (from your headshots), with a headline pulled from the hook.

## What you end up with
A finished, filmable YouTube script in your voice **and** two click-ready thumbnails branded to your channel with your face in them — everything you need to hit record and upload.

## What you need
- **Claude** with this skill installed, and a few **headshots** on first run so the thumbnails look like you.
- **Recommended — a brand-voice skill** so the script sounds like you.
- **For auto-generated thumbnails:** the `gpt-image-2` skill (uses your ChatGPT Plus/Pro + the codex CLI, in Claude Code / desktop). Without it, you still get the two fully-written thumbnail prompts to paste into ChatGPT yourself.
- **Optional — a YouTube Data API feed** for richer trend data. Works fine without it (uses web search).

## Honest limits
It writes the script and designs the thumbnails — it doesn't film or upload for you. Auto-thumbnail generation needs an image tool connected; otherwise you get ready-to-paste prompts. Thumbnails look like you only if you provide real headshots — it never invents a face.
