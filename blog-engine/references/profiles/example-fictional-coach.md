## Voice source
Warm, practical, encouraging but honest. Plain language, short sentences. No hype,
no jargon, no "unlock your potential" filler. Hard bans: fake urgency, shaming the
reader, guaranteeing outcomes.

## The business
Bright Path helps first-time managers lead teams without burning out. Core
mechanism: a 90-day leadership reset built around weekly 1:1 systems. Author:
a former operations director who coached 200+ new managers.

## Who reads the blog
Newly promoted managers (0–2 years in the role) who feel out of their depth and
are quietly googling "how do I run a 1:1" at 11pm. Pain: impostor feeling, no
playbook, inherited a struggling team.

## Topic territory
new manager tips, first 90 days as a manager, how to run a 1:1, giving feedback,
delegation for new leaders, manager burnout. Proprietary terms: "the 90-Day
Reset," "the Weekly 1:1 System."
